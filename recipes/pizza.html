Pizza

Description

A hot, cheesy slice of pizza with a crispy crust, tangy tomato sauce, and your favorite toppings — pure happiness in every bite!

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Cheese
  • Tomato
  • Chicken Sausage
  • ketchup

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough: Mix flour, yeast, salt, water, and a little olive oil. Knead the dough until smooth and let it rise for about an hour.
  2. Roll and Shape: Once risen, roll out the dough on a floured surface into your desired pizza shape and thickness.
  3. Add the Sauce: Spread a generous layer of tomato sauce or pizza base sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Top It Off: Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese and add your favorite toppings like veggies, pepperoni, mushrooms, or olives.
  5. Bake and Serve: Preheat your oven to 220°C (425°F), bake the pizza for 12–15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling. Slice and enjoy!
